Citizen-Q

Citizen-Q will introduce you to quantum physics by playing a computer game, which will help to develop an intuition for quantum effects. In the final stage of the game, you will compete against mathematical methods to implement quantum operations on a real-world quantum computer.

Citizen-Q is a participant driven research project that will help us to understand how debugging a quantum computer feels to a layman. Also, you can help us to increase the quality of current computations on our quantum computer prototype.
