All about

Citizen-Q

Citizen-Q is a participant driven research project that will help us to understand how laymen can understand, operate, and optimize a quantum computer. Citizen-Q will introduce you to quantum physics by playing a computer game, which will help you to develop an intuition for quantum effects and us to build a better quantum computer.

By playing the game, you will help us to increase the quality of current computations on our quantum computer prototype. In the final stage of the game, you will compete against mathematical methods to implement quantum operations on a real-world quantum computer.
