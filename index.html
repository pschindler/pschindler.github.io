Biography

I am a researcher working on experimental approaches towards quantum information processing with trapped ions in the group of Rainer Blatt at the University of Innsbruck. We are investigating new ways of performing computations with single atoms. Our experiments usually consist of implementing algorithms in a small scale prototype as well as determining scalable architectures for quantum information processing with trapped ions.
